Internet of Things(IoT) for Medical Healthcare.

WHAT WE ARE

Internet of Things(IoT) for Medical Healthcare is a B.E. Project by Krish Moodbidri and Kautilya Save under the guidance of Prof. Aruna Gawade.

It encapsulates medical healthcare and the Internet of Things in order to reduce the work of a nurse as well as send alert messages in case of abnormalities wrt users' pulse and temperature.

Register

Sign In

03

Regularly Monitor

Monitor your pulse rate and body temperature at regular intervals.
