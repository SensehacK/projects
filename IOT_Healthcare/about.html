About IoT for Medical Healthcare

Scope of the project

A smart band which is based on Arduino Uno as our hardware board which connects health related sensors to send information on the server. It aims for clinical patients who want to track their daily fitness information. The user can easily distinguish his personal analysis with comparison to the normal consensus within his age group. The system will track the pulse rate within every 10 minutes & will send the data to the Internet on the servers for analysis purposes. The history of the data could be analyzed on the website. If any catastrophic result has been found then the server would immediately send the request to predefined doctors for attending the patient in real life. So the doctors & the healthcare medics would have less overhead & be prepared with more agile solution.
