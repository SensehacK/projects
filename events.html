Icon Speeches

Icon speeches is an even through which the stalwarts of the chemical fraternity will galvanize the youth and inspire them to forge a new path for progress. Veterams of the chemical industry will speak about their experiences, the challenges in chemical engineering and how to face them, and most importantly, the spirit of technical genius and ambition.

Technical Paper Presentation

Technical Paper Presentations are a chance for students as well as professionals to demonstrate their communication skills on a formal scale. A 10 minute duration, along with the assistance of a PPT, is given for the students to provide the panel with an exhaust analysis of their research paper. The victor of the competition is chosen on the basis of their presentation skills, confidence, and also their ability to answer in the cross-questioning session.

Technical Poster Presentation

The best way to explain ones ideas is through a well-prepared poster, and the same thing applies to engineering and research papers. In the Poster Presentation Competition, the participants prepare the poster detailing the paper that they have chosen, and the judges will score them on the effectiveness of their communication and confidence in answering questions.
